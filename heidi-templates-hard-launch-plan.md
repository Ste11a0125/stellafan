# Heidi Templates — Hard Launch Plan
**Scope:** Day 0 through Week 2
**Features:** Version History, Chat Request, Template Editing improvements

---

## Channels

| # | Channel | Role |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | In-app (modal + banner + tooltip) | First-touch at login; persistent catch for dismissers; in-product discovery |
| 2 | Email (admin segment) | Primary activation driver; personalised deep-link |
| 3 | CS / account team | High-touch for enterprise accounts |
| 4 | Changelog / product update page | Shareable reference; CS support tool |
| 5 | Community / LinkedIn | Secondary reach; signals momentum |

---

## Day 0 — Launch

### In-app
- Modal fires on first login for all paying users
  - Headline: what's new in three bullet points (version history, chat request, editing improvements)
  - Primary CTA: `Open my Templates →` — deep-links to their Templates section
  - Secondary: `Remind me later` (triggers persistent banner)
- Persistent banner goes live in the Templates section for users who dismiss the modal

### Email
- Launch email sends to all admin-role users on paid plans
- **Personalised subject line:** `Your [Template Name] template just got easier to manage`
- **Personalised body line:** `Your last edit to [Template Name] on [Date] is already saved in version history — nothing you've built is at risk.`
- Primary CTA: `See your saved versions →` — deep-links directly to that template's version history (not the Templates homepage)
- A/B test subject line variant: `New in Heidi: version history, chat requests, and smarter editing`

### Changelog
- Product update page goes live with three capabilities listed clearly
- Shareable URL distributed to CS team and used in social post
- Support team briefed — changelog is the go-to reference for inbound questions

### CS
- Account team sends a personal note to top 20–30 enterprise accounts
- One sentence, their name, direct offer: `Happy to walk you through what's new in Templates — takes 15 minutes`
- Not a broadcast; sent individually from the account owner

### Community / LinkedIn
- Single post goes live linking to changelog
- Product-update tone, not hype
- No inflated claims

---

## Week 1 — Reinforce

### In-app
- Banner remains live for users who dismissed the modal
- **Chat request activation:** after any user opens a template, a tooltip fires on the chat input area:
  `New: Ask Heidi to rewrite, tighten, or restructure this template. Try it →`
  *(fires once per user, dismissible)*
- **Version history confirmation:** after a user makes their first edit, a lightweight tooltip fires:
  `This version is saved. You can restore it any time.`
  — makes the benefit tangible at the exact moment it matters

### Email
- Monitor open and click rates by Day 3
- Flag any deliverability issues early
- No new sends this week — let the launch email breathe

### CS
- Follow up with enterprise accounts who haven't responded to Day 0 note
- Offer 15-minute walk-through
- Log all friction points and questions — these feed the Week 2 follow-up email content

### Changelog
- Any common inbound question gets added as a FAQ entry
- No other changes

### Community / LinkedIn
- If engagement on the Day 0 post is meaningful, share one early reaction or use case
- Only if organic — do not force

---

## Week 2 — Convert the rest

### In-app
- Feedback prompt fires after a user's first template edit:
  `How easy was that?` — one-tap response (thumbs up / thumbs down)
- Surfaces friction early; positive signal feeds future marketing

### Email
- **Follow-up email** to non-openers and non-clickers from the Day 0 send
- Shorter, one paragraph, one button
- Subject: `Still haven't tried the new Heidi Templates?`
- No personalisation token needed — keep it simple

### CS
- Check in with enterprise accounts post walk-through
- Any positive reactions flagged as candidate testimonials or case study seeds
- Friction themes from Week 1 logs shared with Product

### Metrics review (end of Week 2)

| Metric | What it tells you |
|---|---|
| % of admin users who visited Templates | Reach of launch |
| % who made a first edit | Activation rate |
| % who triggered version history or chat request | Feature discovery rate |
| Modal click-through vs. dismissal rate | In-app message quality |
| Email open rate + click-to-visit rate | Email effectiveness |
| Follow-up email lift over Day 0 | Value of re-engagement send |

## Activation summary

| Moment | Feature | Channel | Psychological lever |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-click (email) | Version history | Personalised email | Loss reduction — "your work is already safe" |
| Post-click (in-product) | Chat request | Tooltip on chat input | Curiosity / gain — "try asking Heidi to improve this" |
| First edit | Version history | In-product tooltip | Confirmation — "this version is saved" |
| End of Week 2 | Both | Feedback prompt | Voice — makes the user feel heard |
